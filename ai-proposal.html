water_drop Water Treatment Technologies

UFUltrafiltration

Membrane filtration removing particles & bacteria (0.01-0.1 µm).

MBRMembrane Bioreactor

Biological treatment + membrane filtration for wastewater.

ROReverse Osmosis

High-pressure removal of dissolved salts (97%+ rejection).

UVUV Disinfection

Pathogen inactivation using ultraviolet light.

AOPAdvanced Oxidation

Hydroxyl radicals break down organic micropollutants.

NFNanofiltration

Between UF and RO, removes divalent ions and organics.

science Water Quality Parameters

TSSTotal Suspended Solids

Particles in water, measured in mg/L.

TDSTotal Dissolved Solids

Dissolved minerals and salts, measured in mg/L.

BODBiochemical Oxygen Demand

Organic pollution indicator (biodegradable).

CODChemical Oxygen Demand

Total oxidizable matter including BOD.

SESpecific Energy

Energy per unit water treated (kWh/m³).

business Business & Commercial

CAPEXCapital Expenditure

Upfront equipment and installation costs.

OPEXOperational Expenditure

Ongoing running costs (energy, chemicals, maintenance).

BOOBuild-Own-Operate

Provider builds, owns, and operates the system.

BOTBuild-Operate-Transfer

Ownership transfers to client after contract period.

TCOTotal Cost of Ownership

Lifetime cost: CAPEX + OPEX over system life.
