# Membracon UK - Business Analysis

## Overview

Membracon UK is a **water treatment systems integrator** specializing in Reverse Osmosis (RO) solutions. They design, source, assemble, install, and commission packaged RO systems for industrial and commercial clients.

---

## Sales Process Workflow

The business operates with a two-stage quotation process:

```
┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│                        MEMBRACON SALES PROCESS                              │
├─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│                                                                             │
│  STAGE 1: Initial Quotation          STAGE 2: Detailed Project Estimate    │
│  ─────────────────────────           ──────────────────────────────────    │
│  "Packaged RO Estimator"      →      Project-specific Quote (e.g. E4924A)  │
│                                                                             │
│  • Customer requirements              • Specific supplier quotes            │
│  • Quick system sizing                • Detailed BOM with part numbers      │
│  • Budgetary pricing                  • Labour breakdown                    │
│  • Feasibility check                  • Project management tracking         │
│  • Alarm/warning flags                • Payment milestones                  │
│                                                                             │
└─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘
```

### Stage 1: Packaged RO Estimator

A configurable tool that takes customer water quality parameters and automatically:
- Sizes the RO system (membranes, vessels, pumps)
- Selects required pre-treatment based on feed water quality
- Calculates pricing with markup
- Displays warnings for problematic water conditions
- Estimates ROI and payback period

### Stage 2: Detailed Project Estimate

Once an opportunity is qualified, a detailed estimate is created with:
- Line-by-line equipment pricing
- Supplier part numbers and PO tracking
- Labour costs (mechanical, electrical, travel, accommodation)
- Project milestones and payment schedule
- Margin analysis

## Business Model

Membracon operates as a **value-added systems integrator**:

1. **Design** - Custom RO systems based on customer water quality and flow requirements
2. **Source** - Components from multiple specialist suppliers
3. **Assemble** - Packaged systems (optionally containerized)
4. **Install** - On-site mechanical and electrical work
5. **Commission** - System startup and handover

### Pre-Treatment Packages

Based on feed water quality, systems may require:

| Water Issue | Treatment | Trigger Condition |
|-------------|-----------|-------------------|
| Chlorine | Carbon filtration | > 0.05 ppm |
| High iron | Iron removal system | > 0.1 mg/L |
| Hard water | Antiscalant dosing | 100-300 mg/L CaCO₃ |
| Very hard water | Softener | > 300 mg/L CaCO₃ |
| Low pH | pH adjustment dosing | < 6 |
| High turbidity | Sand/multimedia filter | > 5 NTU |
| High SDI | Pre-filtration | > 6 |

### Additional Options

- **UV disinfection** - Post-treatment sterilization
- **CIP (Clean-In-Place)** - £3,500 - £4,716 depending on system size
- **Second stage RO** - For ultra-pure water (< 10 µS/cm conductivity)
- **Permeate flushing** - Membrane preservation option
- **Buffer/storage tanks** - Various sizes available

## Feed Water Specification Guidelines

### Iron Levels (mg/L)

| Range | Action |
|-------|--------|
| < 0.02 | Cartridge filter only |
| 0.02 – 0.05 | Fine filtration + monitoring |
| 0.05 – 0.1 | Iron removal recommended |
| > 0.1 | Mandatory iron removal |
| > 0.3 | Dedicated iron removal system |

### Hardness (mg/L as CaCO₃)

| Range | Classification | Action |
|-------|----------------|--------|
| < 100 | Soft | RO only |
| 100 - 200 | Moderately hard | Antiscalant dosing |
| 200 - 300 | Hard | Antiscalant + lower recovery |
| > 300 | Very hard | Softener or hardness removal |

### Other Parameters

| Parameter | Acceptable | Action Required |
|-----------|------------|-----------------|
| Turbidity | < 1 NTU | Standard 5µm cartridge |
| Turbidity | > 5 NTU | Sand filter + cartridge |
| TSS | ≤ 1 mg/L | Standard filtration |
| TSS | > 5 mg/L | Full pre-treatment |
| TDS | ≤ 500 mg/L | Standard membranes |
| TDS | > 1000 mg/L | High-rejection membranes |
| SDI | ≤ 5 | Standard cartridge |
| SDI | > 6 | Pre-treatment required |
| pH | 6 - 8.5 | Standard operation |
| pH | < 5 or > 9 | Risk of membrane damage |
| Feed Pressure | < 3 bar | Boost pump needed |

## System Warnings/Alarms

The estimator tool flags potential issues:

- Temperature too low - may affect flow rate
- Margin too low - pricing review needed
- Conductivity < 10 µS/cm - second stage RO required
- SDI high - pre-treatment required, fouling risk
- TDS high - special membranes needed
- TSS high - full pre-treatment required
- Turbidity high - sand/multimedia filtration needed
- Pressure too low - boost pump needed
- Iron high - dedicated removal system
- pH out of range - membrane damage risk
- Chlorine present - carbon filtration mandatory
- Hard water - antiscalant or softener required
