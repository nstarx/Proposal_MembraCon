# Membracon Business Process Overview

## Company at a Glance

**Membracon** is a UK water technology company headquartered in Wolverhampton with an 8,000 sq ft fabrication facility in Willenhall. They deliver integrated water treatment solutions through in-house manufacture and system integration.

---

## Business Process Flow

Membracon adopts a structured, step-by-step approach to delivering complex water treatment solutions:

```
[1] Modelling & Technical Assessment
            ↓
[2] Lab-Scale Testing & Verification
            ↓
[3] Pilot-Scale Validation
            ↓
[4] Detailed Design & Engineering
            ↓
[5] Full-Scale Build
            ↓
[6] Service & Lifecycle Support
```

### Phase 1: Modelling & Technical Assessment
- Detailed process modelling and technical evaluation
- Understand the challenge
- Define performance targets
- Identify viable treatment pathways

### Phase 2: Lab-Scale Testing & Verification
- Representative samples tested through selected lab-scale systems
- Validate initial assessments
- Assess treatability
- Generate data for accurate system selection for full-scale design

### Phase 3: Pilot-Scale Validation
- Pilot systems deployed onsite
- Prove performance under real operating conditions
- Refine system design
- Build operational confidence

### Phase 4: Detailed Design & Engineering
- Using verified data, develop detailed application-specific design
- Optimized for performance, reliability, and long-term operation

### Phase 5: Full-Scale Build
- Systems manufactured, installed, and commissioned by Membracon
- Ensuring continuity from concept through to operational handover

### Phase 6: Service & Lifecycle Support
- Ongoing service, optimization, and technical support
- Ensure systems operate efficiently and reliably
- Peak performance throughout their lifecycle

- Real-time microbial and process data integration

## Proven Results (Case Studies)

### Water Utilities - Containerised Pilot Plant
- **Need**: Trial ceramic membrane technology at multiple sites
- **Solution**: Two 20ft containerised pilot plants, CIP, remote monitoring
- **Result**: 300,000 L/day per container

### Automotive - Pure Water Supply
- **Need**: Replace obsolete high-energy pure water system
- **Solution**: 16.8m³/hr RO system with variable-speed drives
- **Result**: 403,000 L/day, <50 μS/cm quality, reduced energy use

### Food & Beverage - Sterile Water Production
- **Need**: Replace energy-intensive boiled water process
- **Solution**: 6m³/hr hygienic skid-mounted filtration with UV
- **Result**: 99.99% bacterial removal, >50% energy reduction
